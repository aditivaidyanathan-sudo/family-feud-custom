How to Play

▼
The Main Game
  • Teams take turns competing head-to-head on a question. The survey answers are hidden on the board — teams must guess the most popular responses.
  • The team that wins the face-off (by guessing a top answer first) takes control of the board and tries to reveal all remaining answers.
  • Each correct answer flips a tile and adds its points to the round total.
  • Every wrong answer earns a Strike ✕. Three strikes and the other team gets one chance to Steal — if they guess any unrevealed answer, they take all the points.
  • The host clicks Award on the scoreboard to give the round's points to the winning team.
  • Some rounds carry a ×2 or ×3 multiplier — all answer points are multiplied accordingly.
Strikes & Stealing
  • The team in control answers one at a time. A wrong answer = one strike.
  • After 3 strikes the board locks and the opposing team gets one collective guess to steal.
  • If the steal is correct, they win all accumulated points. If wrong, the controlling team keeps them.
  • The host can award points to any team using the Award buttons on the scoreboard — including after a steal.
Fast Money
  • The top 2 teams with 500+ points after all rounds qualify for Fast Money. If fewer than 2 teams hit 500, the top 2 by score qualify regardless.
  • Each team sends 2 players to the hot seat — one at a time.
  • Player 1 has 20 seconds to answer all 5 questions. Their answers are hidden from Player 2.
  • The board is cleared, then Player 2 has 25 seconds to answer the same 5 questions. They cannot repeat any answer Player 1 already gave.
  • The team's combined score from both players is added to the main scoreboard.
  • Click any answer tile to reveal it, and use the timer controls to start, pause, and reset.
  • The same format repeats for the second qualifying team.
Winning
  • The team with the most total points at the end of Fast Money wins the game.
  • If no teams qualify for Fast Money, the team with the highest score after all rounds wins.
